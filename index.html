About Me

Patricia Swarin is a freelance front-end developer and designer. After graduating in 2003 with a B.S. in Design from Troy University, she joined the team at Square Root Interactive, a web agency focused on enterprise-level content management systems as well as creative digital solutions, from branding to marketing. Patricia learned front-end development at the company, in the glory days of slice and dice, beat-your-head-on-the-wall IE5/Netscape hell.

SRI was a small agency that required one to wear multiple hats, and she became a proponent for web standards in the office and labeled a CSS guru by her peers. Her day-to-day responsibilities included client meetings, creative discoveries, wireframing/prototyping, design, style guides, front-end development and code reviews. It was a collaborative environment where front-end and back-end developers worked side by side through project completion.

She is also focused on professional development and networking. She has served on the board for the American Advertising Federation - Montgomery chapter as communications chair.

As a contractor, she continued freelancing with the agency and eventually Ameex Technologies, a company that absorbed SRI into their fold, in addition to other freelance projects. She has worked with front-end frameworks like Bootstrap. She also has extensive knowledge of Wordpress, Drupal, and Joomla.
